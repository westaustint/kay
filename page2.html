Content Strategy for Tech Startups

View Project Details | March 2025
A comprehensive content strategy developed for an emerging tech startup, focusing on audience engagement, brand consistency, and conversion optimization across digital platforms.
This project involved creating a tailored content strategy that aligned with the client's business objectives while addressing their unique market challenges. Working closely with their marketing team, I developed a framework that integrated SEO best practices, user-centered design principles, and compelling storytelling techniques. The strategy included content calendars, style guides, and performance metrics that allowed for consistent measurement and optimization. The implementation resulted in a 45% increase in user engagement and significantly improved conversion rates across their digital properties.
